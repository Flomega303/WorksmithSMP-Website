Worksmith Information

1️. What is Worksmith SMP?
Worksmith SMP is a unique Minecraft server inspired by Stoneworks, centered around custom factions and thrilling battle experiences. Join us to create your legacy and conquer new frontiers!

2️. What is Our Server IP and Version?
Currently, the server is under development, and the IP will be announced upon release. Stay tuned for updates on our Discord or official website!

3️. Who Can I Ask for More Information?
If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to any of our staff members. They’re here to help and ensure you have the best experience on Worksmith SMP!
